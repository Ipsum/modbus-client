How to determine COM port

  1. Connect the clark Sonic device to your computer and check that it is powered
  2. Click the start button on your computer
  3. Click "Run"
  4. Type: devmgmt.msc
  5. Press enter and expand the "Ports (COM & LPT)" catagory
  6. The COM port which shows up here is the port which the clark Sonic device is using
